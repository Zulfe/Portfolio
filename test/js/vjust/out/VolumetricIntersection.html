VolumetricIntersection

new VolumetricIntersection(north_carvol, east_carvol, south_carvol, west_carvol, north_truckvol, east_truckvol, south_truckvol, west_truckvol, bounded)

Source:
  • classes.js, line 1068
This is a class to organize and contain VolumeTool output data. It accepts DetailedVolume or BoundedDetailedVolume objects and extends the given form to form a record of both. In other words, only DetailedVolume objects or only BoundedDetailedVolume objects may be passed in, but either may be retreived once the object has been instantiated. It is very important that the bounded flag is specified and specified correctly.
Parameters:
Name Type Description
north_carvol DetailedVolume | BoundedDetailedVolume A DetailedVolume or BoundedDetailedVolume for car volumes north or southbound of the intersection
east_carvol DetailedVolume | BoundedDetailedVolume A DetailedVolume or BoundedDetailedVolume for car volumes east or westbound of the intersection
south_carvol DetailedVolume | BoundedDetailedVolume A DetailedVolume or BoundedDetailedVolume for car volumes south or northbound of the intersection
west_carvol DetailedVolume | BoundedDetailedVolume A DetailedVolume or BoundedDetailedVolume for car volumes west or eastbound of the intersection
north_truckvol DetailedVolume | BoundedDetailedVolume A DetailedVolume or BoundedDetailedVolume for truck volumes north or soutbound of the intersection
east_truckvol DetailedVolume | BoundedDetailedVolume A DetailedVolume or BoundedDetailedVolume for truck volumes east or westbound of the intersection
south_truckvol DetailedVolume | BoundedDetailedVolume A DetailedVolume or BoundedDetailedVolume for truck volumes south or northbound of the intersection
west_truckvol DetailedVolume | BoundedDetailedVolume A DetailedVolume or BoundedDetailedVolume for truck volumes west or eastbound of the intersection
bounded boolean A flag specifying if the given volume objects are BoundedDetailedVolume objects
