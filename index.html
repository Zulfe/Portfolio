TDAS - Target Detection and Analysis System

Project Status:

Ongoing


TDAS is a computer vision project that allows skirmishers to better analyze their targets in realtime. The concept is derived from my own experiences as I found it impractical to have a spotting scope to help in accuracy and adjustment if that scope requires that you leave your firing stance, creating a lack of consistency in your shots. TDAS solves this by evaluating shooting bulls for the skirmisher and displaying detected hits on a panel mounted to the spotting scope. Using TDAS, the skirmisher may better understand their target while also being able to remain in their firing stance.
